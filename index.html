I think that neuroscience and astrophysics are alter egos! Aren't there billions of firing stars being studied in both? Unfathomable Universe...Brainverse!

I enjoy doing research in cognitive sciences, biological psychology, neuropharmacology and every subject related to our Brainverse. I currently work as a graduate research and teaching assistant at Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University.

  • Other affiliations in papers: 1) Institute of Biochemistry and Biophysics (IBB), University of Tehran; 2) Dept. Physiology, School of Medicine, Shahid Beheshti University of Medical Sciences (SBMU); 3) Neuroscience Research Center, SBMU; Tehran, Iran.
  • Main research interests:
    • Learning and memory deficits in neurodegeneration (Alzheimer's & Parkinson's disease)
    • Developing tools and protocols to study high-level sensorimotor processing in brain
    • Reward and pain circuitry in brain: molecular architecture and signaling in cortex
    • Machine learning and deep learning in neuroscience, Computer vision
    • Computational neuroscience: Neuroinformatics, Systems neuroscience, Gene network analysis
    • Psychophysics in Attention / Perception
    • Neuropharmacology of atypical antipsychotics (in schizophrenia-related suicide, etc.)
    • Cortical topography of brain: visuotopy, retinotopy, somatotopy
    • Neurobiology of psychiatric and affective disorders
  • Methodology & techniques:
    • Brain electrophysiology (Extracellular Single Unit Recording, Local Field Potential Recording) & Patch clamp
    • Optogenetics
    • Neuroimaging
    • Behavioral paradigms in humans or animal models to study reward circuit and learning & memory in health or disease.
