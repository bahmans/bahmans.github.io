I think that neuroscience and astrophysics are alter egos! Aren't there billions of firing stars being studied in both? Unfathomable!

I enjoy doing research in Cognitive Science, Biological Psychology and Neuropharmacology. I currently work as a research assistant at the Institute of Biochemistry and Biophysics (IBB), University of Tehran.
